Data Source
All the data is collected from Twitter’s REST API. I hope to add Instagram in the near future.
Term Scan

All tweets are scanned for mentions of 3 mealtimes, 933 grape varieties, 45 wine producing regions as well as 168 wine tasting descriptors. I also identify wine colors and blends according to the grape variety mentioned in tweets.
